<p><p>Call Accounting Systems (CAS) can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CAS solutions are hosted on servers accessed via the internet, offering scalability, remote access, and reduced infrastructure costs. They often provide automatic updates and maintenance. On-premises CAS, however, is installed directly on a company's hardware, giving organizations full control over their data and customization options. Each type serves different business needs, balancing convenience with security and control.</p></p>
